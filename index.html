Dari Kami untuk Kita Semua

Bandulan Tea was created by CV. Budi Djaja, a company based in Pekalongan. Our tea has been a staple since 1933, known for its unique flavor and quality.

Our commitment to excellence has made Bandulan Tea a beloved brand across generations. We take pride in our heritage and strive to continue delivering the best tea experience to our customers.

Join us on a journey through the rich history and tradition of Bandulan Tea, and discover why we have been a trusted name in tea for over 90 years.
